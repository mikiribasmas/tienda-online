Mr. Morale & The Big Steppers" is the debut full-length album by the eponymous band, who have been making music together for years.

The album is a collection of songs that reflect their unique style and love for soul and funk music.

The production is impeccable, with a perfect combination of live instruments and electronic sounds that create a dynamic and modern sound.

The lyrics are deep and reflective, covering themes such as perseverance, joy, and hope. Each song tells a clear and moving story.

The vocal arrangements are impressive, with smooth and complex harmonies that perfectly complement the band's music. The choruses add an extra dimension of depth and emotion to each song.

The album includes several upbeat and energetic tracks that will make you move your feet and sing along with the band. There are also some emotive ballads that will touch and inspire you.
